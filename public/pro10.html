About Project

Welcome to Elm at Park Five, a contemporary residential development at Dubai Production City, where modern design meets convenience and comfort. Created by Deyaar, this project offers a collection of exquisite apartments that embody Middle Eastern architectural elegance amidst serene surroundings. Residents will find themselves part of a vibrant community, enriched with world-class amenities from basketball courts to peaceful swimming pools. This forward-thinking development seamlessly integrates urban lifestyles with the tranquility of expansive green landscapes, offering a perfect blend for individuals and families alike. Whether you're looking to relax in a Zen Garden, enjoy recreational activities, or connect with nature in shaded seating areas, Elm Park at Five has something for everyone. The architecture, defined by geometric facades, sets a new standard in residential design, while integrated home automation within each unit ensures a convenient lifestyle. Experience a connected yet peaceful environment, designed to bring the best of both worlds to your doorstep. Step inside and enjoy the perfect setting for cherished moments and new beginnings.

Elm at Park Five isn't just a place to live; it's a thriving community designed for those who seek to live, work, and play in an inspiring environment. With excellent proximity to key areas like Downtown Dubai and Dubai Marina, residents will find themselves conveniently connected to the pulse of the city. The nearby City Centre Me’aisem is just a two-minute drive, offering a plethora of shopping, dining, and entertainment options. The community emphasizes active and leisure pursuits with unique amenities like a running track, tennis court, and steam and sauna facilities. The vibrant marketplace and convenience retail within the development provide everything you need just a short stroll away. With its emphasis on a self-sustaining lifestyle, Elm at Park Five offers an escape from the ordinary, where every corner is designed to enhance your everyday experience. Its strategic location makes it an ideal choice for those searching for a superior living experience in a well-connected community trust that Elm at Park Five will redefine your perspective on modern living.

Prime Connectivity in the Heart of Dubai

Strategically located in Dubai Production City, Elm Park Five offers seamless access to key landmarks and destinations. With Al Maktoum International Airport just 25 minutes away and Downtown Dubai within a 20-minute drive, connectivity is unparalleled. Residents can take advantage of nearby attractions like City Centre Me'aisem, a mere 2-minute drive, or enjoy quick commutes to Dubai Marina, Jumeirah Village Circle, and the Expo 2020 site. Whether traveling for business or leisure, Dubai International Airport is a convenient 30-minute journey. This ensures residents are never too far from the vibrant pulse of the city, while also enjoying the tranquility of their own community. Each journey is effortlessly streamlined, bringing the world closer to your doorstep.

About Developer: DEYAAR

Established in 2002, Deyaar Properties is a leading real estate developer in Dubai, renowned for its luxury properties and innovative developments. Deyaar has a diverse portfolio that includes residential, commercial, and hospitality projects in prime locations such as Business Bay, Dubai Marina, and Al Barsha. Notable projects include Regalia, a 70-story residential tower in Business Bay, and the Midtown development in Dubai Production City. Deyaar's operations are divided into property development, property management, facilities management, and owners’ association management, ensuring comprehensive services and high-quality standards. With over 20 years of experience, Deyaar continues to shape Dubai's urban landscape with state-of-the-art infrastructure and world-class amenities, providing a refined living experience for its residents.
